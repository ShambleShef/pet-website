Meet Your Guides:

Hello! I am Sam's dog Mafalda. I am 15 years old and still cute! I've been living around cats for years and I have some advice for living with our feline friends

  1. Do not try to eat the cat. They may seem delicious, but I promise it won't end well.
  2. Lick the cat at your own risk. Some of them like it, some of them tolerate it and some of them will scratch your eye out.
  3. Cats hate the following things:
    • Water
    • Vacuums (we have this in common!)
    • Being bitten
